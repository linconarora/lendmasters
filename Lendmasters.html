Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Mortgage Broker?

A mortgage broker is a licensed professional who assists individuals in navigating the complexities of home and investment loan financing. At Lend Masters, our concept of mortgage brokers transcends the conventional role, as we strive to offer tailored mortgage broking solutions through our team of seasoned brokers. Acting as intermediaries between you and a diverse network of over 30 major banks and lenders, we aim to foster lasting relationships rather than merely facilitating one-time transactions. In today’s climate, a trusted mortgage broker is more valuable than ever, helping to identify lending policies that suit your circumstances, regardless of prevailing interest rates. This approach saves you considerable time, effort, and money.

What does a Mortgage Broker Do?
A licensed Mortgage Broker plays a versatile role in steering you through the complexities of the loan process. Our assistance goes beyond merely securing loan approvals and preparing documents. We offer personalized services, expertise in refinancing, and access to private banking solutions, all geared toward delivering a finance outcome precisely calibrated to your individual circumstances.
Are Mortgage Brokers Worth It?
Without question, having a Mortgage Broker by your side is invaluable. What distinguishes our team at Lend Masters is our steadfast dedication to top-tier service, deep industry knowledge, favourable deals, and continuous support. With our knowledge and experience, our team not only secures loan approvals but also advocates for your long-term financial objectives.
How can a Mortgage Broker help me?
Mortgage brokers originate two out of every three loans in Australia. Our role is to assist you in securing the best finance for your needs and objectives. At Lend Masters, we take pride in offering lifelong support to help you achieve your financial aspirations.
How do I know which loan is right for me?
Our team of experienced brokers will work closely with you to assess your financial situation, goals, and preferences. Based on this information, we’ll recommend the most suitable loan options that align with your needs and objectives.
What fees are associated with obtaining a loan?
Fees associated with obtaining a loan may include application fees, valuation fees, legal fees, and ongoing fees such as annual or monthly account fees. Our brokers will provide transparency regarding any fees associated with your loan and help you understand the costs involved.
What ongoing support do you provide after the loan is approved?
Our commitment to you doesn’t end once your loan is approved. We provide ongoing support and assistance throughout the life of your loan, including monitoring market conditions, reviewing your financial situation, and exploring opportunities for refinancing or restructuring your loan as needed.
